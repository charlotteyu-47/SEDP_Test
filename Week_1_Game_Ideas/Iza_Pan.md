# Craft & Life

### Game Type
- Sandbox / Open-World Game

- Life Simulation

- Farming & Crafting

- Light Survival

- Single-player with Optional Co-op

### Game description 
- World & Camera

1. 3D voxel-based open world

2. First-person and third-person camera modes

- Core Systems

1. Building System: Free-form block building and interior decoration

2. Farming System: Soil preparation, seasonal crops, animal care

3. Crafting System: Tools, furniture, machines, and upgrades

4. Life System: Day–night cycle, seasons, weather

5. Social System: NPC dialogue, gifting, relationship progression

- Core Gameplay Loop

1. Explore → Gather Resources → Build & Farm → Socialize → Upgrade Skills → Unlock New Areas
